About Us

As high school students, we had a lot of different tests for many different subjects, and we struggled to achieve the best results. After some research, we discovered that we were not the only ones, and that there were several barriers blocking high school students like us from getting the best results from their study.


This lead us to come up with the idea of Oblisk.


The 2 major problems that people face in getting the most out of their study are:


1. Lack of motivation,


and 2. lack of understanding of how to actually study,


So we decided we had to find a solution.


Our solution is simple: create study templates that can be used by anyone. We would like to add video tutorials for these templates. Our product is research backed, customizable and user-friendly. All study templates and materials will link to an educational video that will explain how to use the product effectively and assist people in developing their study skills. Our product will be available as a virtual, online product, but may also be purchased in a hard copy format and packages.


  • Create Study templates that can be used by everyone
  • We want to add linked video tutorials
  • Product research backed, customisable and user-friendly
  • Available virtually as well as physically
